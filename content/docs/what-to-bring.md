## Packing List

Use soft bags rather than hard suitcases — there's little stowage below deck, and soft bags work better in boat (see [Practical Tips](../tips/)). 

Suggested items to bring:

- **Warm layers** — evenings and nights on the water are colder than ashore.
- **Rain gear** — a waterproof jacket and trousers.
- **Soft bags** — they stow far better than rigid cases.
- **Headlamp** — for moving around the boat after dark.
- **Sunscreen** — sun and glare off the water add up quickly.
- **Sleeping bag** — bedding is not provided.
- **Sheets / linen** — and perhaps a small trekking pillow.
- **Drinking water** — bring enough for your trip.
