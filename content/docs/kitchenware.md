## In the Galley

The galley is equipped for simple cooking aboard.

What's on board:

- **Stove** — see [Systems](../systems/) for gas operation.
- **MSR Reactor water boiler** — for fast boiling.
- **Pots and pans** — lightweight, hiking-style.
- **Cups, soup plates, and flat plates** — a set of four of each.
- **Kettle** — for the stovetop.
- **Cutlery** — plastic.
