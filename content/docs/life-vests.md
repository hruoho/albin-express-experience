## Life Jackets

The boat carries six life jackets.

On board:

- **3 × inflatable (gas, "paukkuliivit")**
- **2 × foam, XL**
- **1 × foam, XS/S**

The foam vests are stored in the forwardmost locker.

Spare gas cartridges for the inflatable jackets are in the plastic box behind the toilet bowl.

There are also 2 elastic safety tethers for clipping yourself onto, e.g., the jackstay or toe rail. The inflatable jackets have a loop for this; the foam vests do not.

Check the inflatable jackets before use, and see [Safety Equipment](../safety/) for the rest of the emergency gear.
